02 / Method

Search. Retrieve. Explain.

A strict retrieval-first pipeline. The AI never generates facts — it only summarizes what the database returns. No hallucinations. No generated facts.

Step 01

Search

Queries hit a native SQLite FTS5 index on-device. Arabic, English, and Bengali — instant, even on older phones.

Step 02

Retrieve

Matching verses and hadith are pulled from the local corpus, with surah, book, and number intact. No network request leaves the device.

Step 03

Explain

A local model summarizes only the retrieved passages. If the sources are silent, Hujjah says so.

No hallucinations. No generated facts.  The AI never introduces information outside of what the local database returns.

05 / Trust

A tool you can trust
because nothing leaves.

Hujjah is built by engineers who care about correctness. A research tool for sacred text should not be a surveillance product.

  • 01

    Works offline

    Once installed, Hujjah runs entirely on your device. No connection required, ever.

  • 02

    No external APIs

    Search, retrieval, and explanation all happen locally. There is no server to call.

  • 03

    Your data stays on your device

    No accounts. No telemetry. No backups we can read. The app doesn't know who you are.

  • 04

    Built for accuracy, not speculation

    Explanations are anchored to the text they cite. When the sources are silent, Hujjah says so.
